# ROORQ Drop 001 — Admin Review Checklist

> Use this checklist before approving **every** listing. All 10 points must pass.  
> Drop date: **May 15, 6 PM IST** | Venue: IIT Roorkee marketplace

---

## Checklist (10 Points)

| # | Check | Pass | Fail action |
|---|-------|------|-------------|
| 1 | **Photo count & quality** — minimum 3 photos; at least one flat-lay, one detail shot, one worn/held shot. No blurry or dark images. | ☐ | Request re-shoot |
| 2 | **Story Score accuracy** — score matches visible condition (check stitching, hardware, soles, fabric pilling). Reviewer must assign independently before comparing to seller's score. | ☐ | Correct score; flag if >2 points off seller's claim |
| 3 | **Brand verification** — visible logo/tag in at least one photo. For luxury/streetwear, check stitching, font, and hardware against brand reference images. Mark "Unverified Brand" if uncertain. | ☐ | Add "Unverified Brand" tag or reject |
| 4 | **Title format** — must follow: `[Brand] [Item type] — [Key detail]` e.g. *Levi's 511 Slim — Washed Indigo*. No ALL CAPS, no emojis, no price in title. | ☐ | Edit title before approval |
| 5 | **Price sanity check** — falls within the Category × Condition price band (see Pricing Table). Not below ₹150 floor, not above 65% of verifiable MRP. | ☐ | Request repricing |
| 6 | **Description completeness** — must include: item measurements OR size tag photo, material if visible on label, any flaws called out explicitly (hole, stain, fading). | ☐ | Return to vendor for revision |
| 7 | **No prohibited items** — undergarments, swimwear, socks, face masks, or items with offensive graphics are not eligible for Drop 001. | ☐ | Reject listing |
| 8 | **Authenticity red flags** — check for misspelled brand names, irregular stitching, missing care labels, or suspiciously pristine "pre-loved" luxury items. | ☐ | Flag for secondary review or reject |
| 9 | **Category tagging** — correct primary category (Tops, Bottoms, Outerwear, Footwear, Accessories) and relevant tags (e.g. Y2K, Streetwear, Minimal) assigned. | ☐ | Correct tags |
| 10 | **Drop readiness** — product is physically in hand with the vendor/team (not "will source"), available for same-day handoff, and has not been simultaneously listed on another platform. | ☐ | Remove from drop roster |

## Quick Reference — Story Score Guide

| Score | Meaning | Visible signs |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| 8–10 | Excellent / Near-new | No visible wear; tags may still be attached |
| 5–7  | Good | Light wash fade, minor pilling; no damage |
| 1–4  | Fair | Visible wear, small stains or repairs; must be disclosed |

## Instant Reject Conditions (any one = reject, no revision)

- Counterfeit confirmed
- Damage not disclosed by seller that materially affects use (broken zip, torn seam >3 cm)
- Listed simultaneously on OLX / Depop / Instagram at a different price
- Fewer than 2 usable photos after review
- Item is prohibited (see Point 7)
