Vendor Welcome Guide
Everything you need to know before your first listing.

Welcome to ROORQ

ROORQ is IIT Roorkee's curated pre-loved fashion marketplace. We're not a generic resale app — we're a drop-based platform built specifically for the IITR campus, where every piece listed has a story and a spotlight.

If you have a hoodie you've outgrown, sneakers that deserve a new life, or streetwear sitting in your wardrobe, ROORQ is how you sell it right — to people who actually care about what they're buying.

What Makes ROORQ Different

Curated, not just listed. Every product on ROORQ is reviewed by our team before it goes live. Buyers trust what they see. That trust is why they pay fairly — and why you get more than you would selling on a WhatsApp group.

Drops, not listings. ROORQ releases curated Drops — limited-time events where a batch of reviewed products goes live together. Drops create urgency, demand, and a real moment around your item.

Campus delivery. Zero hassle. You don't pack, ship, or coordinate. ROORQ handles delivery within the IITR campus. You hand over the item, we do the rest.

How Drops Work

  1. You list your product on the vendor dashboard.
  2. ROORQ's team reviews and approves it for the next Drop.
  3. Approved products are queued for the next available Drop.
  4. When the Drop goes live, your item is visible to buyers for a limited window.
  5. If it sells — you get paid. If it doesn't — it may roll into the next Drop or you can relist.

What Makes a Great Listing

Photos (3–6 required)

  • Natural light only — no dark or yellow-tinted shots
  • Front, back, and detail — stitching, logos, tags, any wear marks
  • Flat lay or worn — both work; worn shots tend to convert better
  • Show flaws honestly — a visible flaw in the photo is better than a buyer surprise
  • Clean, neutral background (a white bedsheet works fine)

Title & Brand

Be specific. Not "Blue Hoodie" — write "Nike Tech Fleece Hoodie – Navy, Size M." Always fill in the brand, even if it's a lesser-known label.

Condition

Describe any defects, fading, or alterations honestly in the description field. Our team verifies condition during review.

Price

You set your own asking price. ROORQ may suggest a market-appropriate range, but the final call is yours.

Vendor Quick-Start Checklist
From "I want to sell" to "my product is live" — 6 steps.

Before You Start

Gather these before you open the dashboard:

  • 3–6 clear photos of your item (natural light, show front / back / details / flaws)
  • Item details: brand, category, size, any defects
  • Your asking price in mind
  • UPI ID for receiving payouts

The 6 Steps

1

Create Your Vendor Account

Go to roorq.in/vendor → Sign up with your phone number or IITR email → Verify OTP.

2

Complete Your Profile

Add your name, WhatsApp number, and UPI ID. This is how ROORQ contacts you and sends your payout. Don't skip the UPI step.

3

Add Your Product

Click "Add Product" and fill in:

  • Title (brand + item + colour + size)
  • Category (clothing / sneakers / accessories / other)
  • Brand name
  • Condition description (wear, fading, repairs)
  • Your asking price
  • Upload 3–6 photos

Hit Submit for Review.

4

Wait for ROORQ's Review

Our team checks your submission and approves it — usually within 48 hours. You'll get a WhatsApp message when it's done.

  • Keep WhatsApp notifications on so you don't miss the update.
5

Get Approved → Queued for Drop

Once approved, your product status changes to "Scheduled" on your dashboard. It's now in the queue for the next Drop.

  • Check your dashboard to confirm status is "Scheduled."
6

Drop Goes Live → You're Selling

When the Drop launches, your item is visible to buyers on ROORQ. You'll get notified if an order comes in.

  • Keep the item ready and accessible for handover.
  • Respond to any ROORQ coordination messages promptly.

Vendor FAQ
The questions every vendor asks — answered.
1. When do I get paid?
Within 7 days of confirmed delivery. Once the buyer receives the item and delivery is confirmed, ROORQ initiates a UPI transfer of 90% of the sale price to the UPI ID on your vendor profile.
2. What if my item doesn't sell during a Drop?
No problem. If your product doesn't sell in a Drop, it's eligible to be included in the next Drop. You'll see its status on your dashboard and can choose to keep it listed, adjust the price, or withdraw it. You're never locked in — you can delist at any time before it sells.
3. Who handles delivery?
ROORQ does. You don't ship anything. When an order is placed, our team coordinates pickup and campus delivery directly. We'll reach out via WhatsApp to arrange handover. All you need to do is have the item ready.
4. Can I set my own price?
Yes. You set the asking price for your item. ROORQ may suggest a range based on the item's category and condition, but the final decision is yours.
5. What if my product gets rejected?
If ROORQ's team finds the item is not a good fit — due to photo quality, unclear condition, or category restrictions — you'll receive a WhatsApp message explaining why and what to fix. You can resubmit after making changes. Rejection isn't permanent; it's feedback.
6. Do I pay anything upfront to list?
No. There are no listing fees, no registration fees, no upfront charges of any kind. ROORQ only takes a commission when your item actually sells (10%). If it doesn't sell, you pay nothing.
7. How does payment to the buyer work?
Buyers pay at doorstep — either Cash on Delivery or UPI — when ROORQ's team delivers the item. ROORQ collects on your behalf and remits 90% to you after delivery confirmation. You never handle buyer payments or chase anyone for money.
8. What types of items can I sell?
ROORQ accepts: Clothing (tops, bottoms, outerwear, ethnic wear, activewear), Footwear (sneakers, boots, sandals), Accessories (bags, caps, belts, jewellery, sunglasses), and Streetwear / branded fashion.

We do not accept innerwear, swimwear, heavily damaged items, or counterfeits. If you're unsure, just WhatsApp us.
9. How long does the review process take?
Usually within 48 hours of submission. During peak periods it may take up to 72 hours. You'll always receive a WhatsApp notification when your product is reviewed — approved, declined, or if we need more photos.
