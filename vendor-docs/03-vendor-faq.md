# ROORQ Vendor FAQ

**The 10 questions every vendor asks.**

---

### 1. When do I get paid?

You get paid within **7 days of confirmed delivery**. Once the buyer receives the item and delivery is confirmed, ROORQ initiates a UPI transfer of **90% of the sale price** to the UPI ID on your vendor profile.

---

### 2. What if my item doesn't sell during a Drop?

No problem. If your product doesn't sell in a Drop, it doesn't disappear — it's eligible to be included in the **next Drop**. You'll see its status on your dashboard and can choose to keep it listed, adjust the price, or withdraw it.

You're never locked in. You can delist your product at any time before it sells.

---

### 3. Who handles delivery?

**ROORQ does.** You don't ship anything. When an order is placed, our team coordinates the pickup and campus delivery directly. We'll reach out via WhatsApp to arrange handover of the item. All you need to do is have the item ready.

---

### 4. Can I set my own price?

Yes. You set the asking price for your item. ROORQ may suggest a range based on the item's category and Story Score, but the final decision is yours.

Keep in mind: the Story Score is visible to buyers and directly affects demand. A fairly priced item with a high Story Score sells fastest.

---

### 5. What does the Story Score mean?

The Story Score is ROORQ's **condition rating on a scale of 1–10**, assigned by our review team after inspecting your submitted photos and description.

| Score | What it means |
|-------|--------------|
| 9–10 | Near mint — barely worn, no visible flaws |
| 7–8 | Light use — minor signs of wear, fully functional |
| 5–6 | Normal wear — visible use but nothing structural |
| 3–4 | Heavy wear — notable flaws clearly disclosed |
| 1–2 | Project piece — significant wear, priced accordingly |

A high score isn't required to sell — buyers specifically look for lower-scored items at lower prices. Honest scoring builds buyer trust and protects you from disputes.

---

### 6. What if my product gets rejected?

If ROORQ's team finds the item is not a good fit — due to photo quality, unclear condition, or category restrictions — you'll receive a WhatsApp message explaining why and what to fix. You can resubmit after making the suggested changes. Rejection isn't permanent; it's feedback.

---

### 7. Do I pay anything upfront to list?

No. There are **no listing fees, no registration fees, no upfront charges** of any kind. ROORQ only takes a commission when your item actually sells (10% of the sale price). If it doesn't sell, you pay nothing.

---

### 8. How does payment to the buyer work?

Buyers pay at doorstep — either **Cash on Delivery** or **UPI** — when ROORQ's team delivers the item. ROORQ collects the payment on your behalf and remits 90% to you after delivery confirmation.

You never handle buyer payments or chase anyone for money.

---

### 9. What types of items can I sell?

ROORQ accepts:
- Clothing (tops, bottoms, outerwear, ethnic wear, activewear)
- Footwear (sneakers, boots, sandals)
- Accessories (bags, caps, belts, jewellery, sunglasses)
- Streetwear and branded fashion

We **do not accept** innerwear, swimwear, heavily damaged items, or counterfeits. If you're unsure whether your item qualifies, just WhatsApp us — we'll tell you.

---

### 10. How long does the review process take?

Usually **within 48 hours** of submission. During peak periods (right before a Drop launches), it may take up to 72 hours. You'll always receive a WhatsApp notification when your product is reviewed — approved, declined, or if we need more photos.
